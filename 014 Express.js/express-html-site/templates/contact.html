Frequently Asked Questions

What are your membership options?

We offer Basic ($29/month), Premium ($59/month), and Elite ($99/month) memberships. Each tier includes different benefits and access levels.

Do you offer free trials?

Yes! We offer a complimentary day pass for first-time visitors. Contact us to schedule your free visit.

Are personal trainers included?

Personal training sessions are included with Premium (1/month) and Elite (4/month) memberships. Additional sessions can be purchased.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Bring a valid ID, water bottle, workout clothes, and athletic shoes. We provide towels and have lockers available.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have free parking for all members with over 200 spots available.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons.
